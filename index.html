Melanie 'Miki' Sapiandante

About me

Melanie, aka Miki, is a first generation queer filipinx residing in Philadelphia. Pronouns are anything said with respect and/or love. Melanie's work histories are in social work and healthcare and most recently in the food industry. Outside of work some of Melanie's interests are good food, social justice, poetry and laughter.
